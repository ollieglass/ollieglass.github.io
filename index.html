Data science, machine learning & data strategy

ollie@ollieglass.com ~ @ollieglass ~ LinkedIn

I create commercial machine learning systems and strategies for companies looking to drive growth with data.

My work spans data science, engineering, product and strategy. Clients include venture-backed startups funded by Index Ventures, Balderton Capital, BGF and other top investors.

My work includes:

  • Identifying growth opportunities made possible by AI, machine learning and data science.
  • Developing early stage ideas into finished data products
  • Testing and clarifying ideas with experiments and prototypes
  • Creating roadmaps for data products and programs
  • Analysing data and communicating findings
  • Building, leading and transforming data teams
  • Advocating for data science and machine learning within businesses
  • Working with stakeholders across all functions and levels

If you're looking for a data scientist, if you have a project in mind or want to explore ideas, feel free to get in touch ollie@ollieglass.com. I love talking to startups and about data in general, so don't hesitate to email if there's something I might be able to help out with.
